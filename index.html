Chicken Parmesan



Recipe Introduction

Chicken parmeson, or chicken parmigiana, combines three of the best foods one could ask for in a dish: breaded chicken, parmesan cheese, and tomato sauce.

It is also as versatile as it is delicious. Any recipe, including the one found on this site, can easily have ingredients substituted to match any budget or preference.

But more importantly, chicken parmeson is a dish that can easily and quickly be put together by even the most novice of chefs (like myself).
